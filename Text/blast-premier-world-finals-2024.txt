INTRO BODY:
  KIN, in collaboration with BLAST and Redd+E, co-organised the BLAST
  Premier World Finals 2024 — marking the first time this global esports
  championship was held in Southeast Asia. Held from October 30 to
  November 3 at Resorts World Sentosa, the event drew participants and
  spectators from around the world.

  The project was more than a competition: it was a landmark moment for
  local and regional esports communities. KIN's objectives were clear —
  introduce a global-level esports event to the region, create an
  unparalleled spectacle, and contribute to the growth of esports in
  Singapore.

BODY:
  ### The Objectives
  KIN set out with three goals: introduce a global-level esports event to
  Southeast Asia, creating a landmark moment for local and regional
  communities; deliver an unparalleled esports spectacle with intense
  competition and unforgettable experiences; and contribute to the growth
  of esports in Singapore by igniting industry-wide development and
  supporting a vibrant, inclusive community.

  ### Partnership and Approach
  By teaming up with BLAST and Redd+E, KIN leveraged combined expertise to
  ensure a high-quality event. The focus was on creating a unique and
  engaging experience — from immersive event design to the selection of
  competitive games that appealed to a wide audience. Extensive efforts
  were made to involve local and regional esports communities through
  interactive activities and inclusive participatory opportunities.

  ### The Event
  The five-day event featured top-tier esports competitions, with activities
  designed to maximise engagement and provide entertainment beyond the
  matches. The event attracted attendees both in-person and virtually from
  across the globe, with a significant increase in local and regional
  esports community engagement.

  ### Impact
  The successful execution has been credited with setting a new benchmark
  for esports events in Southeast Asia, propelling Singapore into the
  spotlight as a key player in the global esports arena. Through strategic
  partnerships, innovative planning, and a commitment to community
  engagement, KIN established a new standard for esports events in the
  region.
