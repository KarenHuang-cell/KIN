INTRO BODY:
  The HSBC SVNS Singapore 2024 marked an exciting new chapter in sevens
  rugby, taking the sport to unprecedented heights with a unique blend of
  athleticism, entertainment, and cultural experiences. Held from May 3–5
  at the iconic Singapore National Stadium, the event brought together
  fans, athletes, and enthusiasts for a thrilling three-day celebration.

  More than just a rugby tournament, it was a festival blending sport,
  music, food, fitness, and immersive experiences. Over 44,000 spectators
  flocked to the stadium to witness top-tier action featuring 12 men's
  and 12 women's teams from powerhouse nations including New Zealand, Fiji,
  Australia, Argentina, Great Britain, and the USA.

BODY:
  ### The Fan Experience
  Fans were treated to a diverse array of experiences off the pitch. The
  Food Market at the 100Plus Promenade offered dishes from local food trucks,
  while the Sun Stage provided non-stop music and performances. The Beach
  Club stood out as a fan favourite — a relaxed atmosphere with beverages
  and DJ music while watching the matches. For corporate guests and VIPs,
  the Sunroom Lounge provided curated entertainment, gourmet food, and
  big-screen action. The event also featured finals for School 7s, SEA 7s,
  and Titans Ruck and Rumble, plus the Turf Games fitness competition.

  ### Venue and Operations
  KIN's scope encompassed meticulously planned use of venue space — venue
  dressing, client flow, signage, and access control for both human and
  vehicle traffic. Comprehensive risk assessment and contingency plans
  ensured smooth operations throughout. Customised builds and general
  overlays were provided for operational and experiential areas both on
  and off the pitch, including Turf Games, Merchandise Booth, Experiential
  Zone, and corporate boxes.

  ### Auxiliary and Logistics
  Auxiliary services — security, cleaning, gate operations, competition
  medical services, and internet provision — were coordinated to keep both
  Front of House and Back of House running smoothly. All logistical aspects
  for players and officials, including arrival, departure, accommodation,
  accreditation, and local transportation, were managed with the assistance
  of 50 Team Liaison Officers.

  ### Food, Beverage, and Volunteers
  KIN coordinated food and beverage operations spanning crew meals, player
  and official menus, and all hydration requirements. Volunteers were
  recruited, briefed, and deployed across various roles to ensure smooth
  execution throughout the three-day event.

  ### Working with World Rugby
  Despite it being the first time working directly with World Rugby
  Tournament Limited, KIN navigated budgetary uncertainties and coordinated
  with foreign agencies unfamiliar with the local market. By gathering local
  industry experts and sourcing materials from China, costs were reduced
  without compromising quality. A team was also sent to HSBC SVNS Perth to
  gain insights into the new event model ahead of the Singapore games.
