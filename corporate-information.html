Leong Yue Kheong

Lead Independent Director

Appointed as Lead Independent Director on 30 March 2026, Mr Leong Yue Kheong brings extensive leadership experience across tourism, public sector development, and strategic planning. He is the founder of 3 Queens, a consulting firm specialising in coaching, mentoring, and tourism master planning.

Mr Leong previously served as Deputy Chief Executive Officer (Development) of Mandai Park Development Pte. Ltd. where he led the planning and execution of key developments within the Mandai precinct, and ecotourism including Bird Paradise and the Mandai Rainforest Resort.

Raymond Lee

Group Financial Controller

Mr Raymond Lee joined the Group in January 2025 and is responsible for overseeing the Group's financial reporting, audit treasury, tax, mergers and acquisitions, and internal control functions. Mr Lee has significant experience in financial management with SGX-listed companies.

Prior to joining the Group, Mr Lee served as Group Financial Controller at Catalist-listed Longbowe Health Limited and oversaw administration and financial roles at Hytra, Altis Holdings, SUTL Enterprise Limited and 8Telecom International Holdings. Mr Lee is also a Registered Member and has provided firms with financial reporting standards and compliance matters throughout his career and is the SOA-21.

Mr Lee started his career as an auditor at BDO LLP. He holds a Master of Business Administration from Curtin University of Australia, and a Bachelor of Commerce (Accountancy, Honours) from the Nanyang Technological University.
